qk.rs

    qk.rs (pronounced “Quackers”) helps you search websites quicker. Connected to over 100 websites and offering user-friendly shortcuts, the platform provides real-time suggestions as you type. Every search generates a shareable link. For example, you can search for hats on Amazon by typing "a hats," resulting in the shareable link "a.qk.rs/hats." Similarly, typing "w" retrieves local weather information. A comprehensive cheat sheet is available, outlining all available website options. It’s important to note that qk.rs prioritizes user privacy by abstaining from collecting any personal information, and no account sign-in is required. What are you waiting for? Search quicker.
