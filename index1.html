qk.rs

    qk.rs (pronounced “Quackers”) helps you search websites quicker. Connected to websites and offering user-friendly shortcuts, this platform provides real-time suggestions as you type. Every search includes a shareable link (just click the link above the search box to copy it). Imagine a search for hats on Amazon. Typing "a hats," results in the shareable link: a.qk.rs/hats Typing "w" then enter, retrieves local weather information. A comprehensive website cheat sheet is available, outlining all available website options. It’s important to note that qk.rs prioritizes user privacy by abstaining from collecting any personal information, and no sign-in is required. What are you waiting for? Search quicker.
